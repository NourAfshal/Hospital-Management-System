    Cardiology

    The Cardiology Department at Medicio Hospital provides comprehensive care to a wide spectrum of patients with cardiovascular diseases.

    Doctors along with a dedicated team of nursing specialists, technicians and staff cover the clinical services which include:

    • - Noninvasive services including Electrocardiogram ECG, Holter monitoring, Stress Test and Echo Doppler (cardiac and transesophageal).
    • - Cardiac catheterization laboratory.
    • - Cardiothoracic Surgery.
    • - Cardiac Care Unit.
    • - Cardiac Surgery Unit.

    Neurology

    The neurology department is a part of a medical institution that provides care, and research dor dieases of neurvous system.

    The Departments of Neurology and Neurosurgery provide expert care to thousands of adults and children each year, many with rare, complex conditions. Our team of neurologists and neurosurgeons push the boundaries of what's possible through groundbreaking research and clinical trials.

    Hepatology

    The Renal Dialysis unit runs six days a week from Monday to Saturday, with two patient shifts each day.

    The Renal Dialysis unit provides hemodialysis treatments to a diverse group of adult patients who have both acute and chronic kidney failure. The care is offered by a team of professionals including Nephrologists and well-trained registered nurses.

    Pediatrics

    The Pediatric Department at Medicio Hospital is committed to the well-being of children from 1 month to 15 years of age.

    The pediatric team consists of highly qualified pediatricians and specialized nurses, promoting healthy child growth and child safety, and works to raise awareness amongst parents to reduce the risk of injuries.
